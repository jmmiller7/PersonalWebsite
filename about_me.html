About Me

I have been passionate about technology since the first time I used a computer. I enjoy learning about new breakthroughs in technology and how it affects the future. I also love problem solving and helping to make other peoples’ lives easier.

I am currently a senior at Carroll University with a major in Computer Science and a minor in Management and Leadership. I have been on the Dean’s List for all of my six semesters attending Carroll and currently have a 3.82/4.00 GPA. My favorite courses are without a doubt my programming courses as I love the challenges that they pose in addition to learning new development techniques.

Over the years, I have developed strong communication and interpersonal skills. My professional work ethic proves me to be an ambitious individual with a strong attention to detail. I can adapt to changing situations and am comfortable working both on-site or remote. I also have experience in leadership, customer service and event management.
